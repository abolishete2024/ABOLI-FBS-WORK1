First Rain

Experience of the First Rain

The first rain of the season is always magical. The smell of wet soil brings back sweet memories.
It feels like nature is celebrating after a long summer.

Things I Love During First Rain:

1. Aroma of wet mud
2. Hot tea with snacks
3. Watching raindrops on the window

Favorite Memory:

Dancing in the rain with friends,
Without caring about the world,
Laughing, jumping in puddles —
That’s the joy of the first rain!
Rain and Science:

Rain is part of the water cycle. Water evaporates, forms clouds, and falls back as rain.
Water's formula is H2O, and its boiling point is 100oC.

Conclusion:

The first rain is not just water falling from the sky — it’s emotion, nostalgia, and peace.
